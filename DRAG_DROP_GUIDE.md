## 🎯 STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS:

### Step 1: Open the Folder
1. Open File Explorer
2. Navigate to: `E:\PROJECTS\CYBER\OS-INT\OSINT_WEB`
3. You will see a folder named **`out`**

### Step 2: Go to Netlify Drop
1. Open your browser
2. Go to: https://app.netlify.com/drop
3. Sign in to Netlify (or create a free account)

### Step 3: Drag & Drop
1. **Drag the ENTIRE `out` folder** into the Netlify Drop zone
2. Wait 10-30 seconds for upload
3. **DONE!** Your site is live! 🎉
